## English

### What is Mewmodoro?

Mewmodoro is a Windows desktop application that features a cute cat mascot, **KIKI**, who lives on your screen. Not just a timer, KIKI monitors your active windows and idle time to ensure you stay focused during your work sessions.

### Key Features

- **Desktop Mascot KIKI**: A pixel-art cat that stays on top of your windows.
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Standard 25/5/15 minute cycles to manage your productivity.
- **Strict Monitoring**: KIKI will alert you if you open blacklisted apps (YouTube, Social Media, Games) during focus time.
- **Idle Detection**: Automatically pauses the timer if you step away from your PC.
- **Ghost Mode**: Click-through mode so KIKI doesn't interfere with your work.
- **Pro Features**: Unlock the Shop, ToDo list, and detailed analytics.

### How to Use

1. **Download**: Get the latest `Mewmodoro Setup.exe` from the releases page.
2. **Install**: Run the installer. You may see a "Windows protected your PC" warning. Click "More info" and "Run anyway" to proceed.
3. **Start Focusing**: Click the cat to start the timer and get to work!
